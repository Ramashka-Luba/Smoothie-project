What are the smoothies?

A smoothie is a drink made from pureed raw fruit, vegetables, and sometimes dairy products (milk, yogurt,
ice-cream or cottage cheese), typically using a blender. Smoothies may be made using other ingredients, such
as water, crushed ice, fruit juice, sweeteners (e.g. honey, sugar, stevia, or syrup), whey powder, plant milk, nuts,
nut butter, seeds, tea, chocolate, herbal supplements, or nutritional supplements.
